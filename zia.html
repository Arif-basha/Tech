Welcome to ZiaTech

The foundation of the Zia College of Technology was laid in the year 1994, near Tiruchengode, South India. A vital part of the enormous Zia Group of Institutions, it is carving a distinct place for itself under the tutelage of Arif A ZiaTech is located in a scenic campus area of over 300 acres with a built-up area of 12 lakh square feet. A combination of cutting edge infrastructure and well-accomplished staff makes it a premier center for learning. In fact, it has the ninth-largest intake of students (as per AICTE approval) among the self-financing engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu.
